Overview

Purpose

The purpose of my personal website project is to create an online platform that serves as a digital representation of my identity, showcasing my skills, experiences, and interests. Through this website, i aim to connect, engage, and motivate visitors while i also provide means for potential employers, collaborators, and like-minded individuals to learn more about me and my work.

Audience

The main audience for my personal website project includes potential employers, collaborators,and peers in my field. Additionally, it also targets individuals interested in my design, creativity, and content.
